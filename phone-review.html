iPhone 15 Pro Max vs Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra: The Ultimate Battle

We compare Apple's and Samsung's flagship smartphones to help you choose the best phone of 2024

The battle for smartphone supremacy continues in 2024 with Apple's iPhone 15 Pro Max and Samsung's Galaxy S24 Ultra leading the charge. Both devices represent the pinnacle of mobile technology, but which one deserves your hard-earned money?

Design & Build Quality

The iPhone 15 Pro Max features a titanium frame that's lighter and stronger than previous stainless steel models. Samsung counters with its Armor Aluminum frame and Gorilla Glass Victus 2 protection. Both phones feel premium, but the iPhone's curved edges and matte finish offer a more comfortable grip.

Display Comparison

Feature iPhone 15 Pro Max Samsung S24 Ultra Winner
Size 6.7 inches 6.8 inches Samsung
Resolution 2796 x 1290 3120 x 1440 Samsung
Brightness 2000 nits peak 2600 nits peak Samsung
Refresh Rate 120Hz ProMotion 1-120Hz Adaptive Tie

Performance

Apple's A17 Pro chip powers the iPhone 15 Pro Max, while Samsung uses the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 for Galaxy. Both chips deliver exceptional performance, but the A17 Pro has an edge in single-core tasks and gaming efficiency.

Camera Systems

This is where the battle gets interesting. The iPhone 15 Pro Max features a 48MP main sensor, while the S24 Ultra boasts a massive 200MP main camera. Samsung's zoom capabilities are superior with 10x optical zoom, but iPhone's computational photography often produces more natural-looking photos.

Battery Life & Charging

  • iPhone 15 Pro Max: Up to 29 hours video playback, 27W fast charging, 15W MagSafe
  • Samsung S24 Ultra: Up to 30 hours video playback, 45W fast charging, 15W wireless

Price

The iPhone 15 Pro Max starts at $1,199, while the S24 Ultra begins at $1,299. Both are expensive, but Samsung includes the S Pen and faster charging in the base price.

Final Verdict

Choose iPhone 15 Pro Max if: You value simplicity, ecosystem integration, and video quality.

Choose Samsung S24 Ultra if: You want customization, superior zoom, and productivity features.
